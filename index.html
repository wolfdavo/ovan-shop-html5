Ovan collaborates with gifted artists from around the globe to design jaw-dropping, world class collections with distinct inspiration from each artist. Collections go to auction, where each garment is bid on individually.

Sustainable Practices.

Every Ovan shirt saves at least 4 plastic bottles from going to landfills and polluting our beautiful planet. The recycled rPET fibers are blended with a 100% organic fair trade cotton; resulting in a high quality, sustainable, and comfortable material.
